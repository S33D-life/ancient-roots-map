# Rootstones Distribution Guidance

Target: seed `33` rootstones per country as a balanced, credible starting set.

## Spatial spread
- Aim for an even national spread:
  - `11` north
  - `11` central
  - `11` south
- If a country has different geography, adapt to equivalent regional partitions (e.g. island groups, ecozones, watersheds).

## Mix of rootstone types
- Include a balanced mix across:
  - sacred/cultural landmarks
  - ecological giants / notable individuals
  - protected areas and conservation anchors
  - community landmarks with clear provenance

## Evidence standards
- Keep claims modest unless directly sourced.
- Avoid unsupported age/superlative claims.
- Prioritize source order:
  1. government and protected-area authorities
  2. reputable conservation NGOs
  3. academic institutions and peer-reviewed references

## Metadata hygiene
- Include source name + URL for every record.
- If exact coordinates are not available:
  - provide `place` and/or `mapsUrl`
  - use confidence `medium` or `low`
  - include `needs_coords` tag.
