The Core System

Private memory funds public commons.

Three movements, one ecology. The subscription that preserves a family's story simultaneously funds the permanent public record of the ancient tree it is anchored to.

📚 The Library Private, immutable digital libraries for people, families, and communities. Personal memory infrastructure. This is where S33D earns.
🌳 The Commons A living atlas of ancient trees — mapped, verified, held in common. Open to everyone. Funded by the private libraries above.
💛 The Economy Hearts earned through presence. Seeds planted daily. Value generated when two people meet at a real tree. Not speculation — encounter.

Two things most people deeply want — paid for by one decision.

III
The Four Layers — What a Staff Carries

Four layers. One staff.

Each founding staff is an anchor into four interlocking layers of the S33D ecosystem — presence, memory, economy, and governance.

🌳 Ancient Friends

Ancient trees mapped, verified, and held in common. The root record of the Living Library — each one a living witness to centuries of history, now publicly documented forever.

📚 Heartwood Library

Lifetime personal and family archives, anchored to a living ancient tree. Books, music, journals, seed records, memorial archives, encounter history — private memory infrastructure that grows ring by ring.

💛 S33D Hearts

The planned encounter economy — earned through mapping ancient trees, offerings, and real-world stewardship. Not speculation. Planned for Ethereum; currently stewarded through Papaya Seed Ltd treasury structures.

⭕ Staff Circles & Governance

Species Circles become the long-term governance bodies of the network — stewarding lineage, protocol, and relationships between people and ancient trees. Founding staffs hold permanent placement in the origin spiral.

Not viral growth. Living growth. Ring by ring. Branch by branch. Species by species.
